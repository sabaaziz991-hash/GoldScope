Premium Access

  • GoldScope Pro unlocks additional on-device screening features, coin workflows, imports, exports, purity screening, and saved history.
  • Annual Pro is an auto-renewable subscription billed through Apple.
  • Lifetime Pro is a one-time purchase for current on-device Pro features.
  • Purchases and restore flows are handled through Apple StoreKit.

Subscription Renewal and Cancellation

Annual Pro renews automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your Apple account may be charged for renewal within 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your App Store account settings.
